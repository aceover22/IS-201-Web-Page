BeYOU Retreat

My name is Lacie Conover and I am the host of the BeYOU retreat. I am happy to introduce you to this incredible weekend! See my page to learn more about me.


For two years, I have hosted a summer retreat for teenage girls about confidence. Each year we have speakers, couslers and activities to help the girls learn how to build confidence. Many teeangers struggle with confidence which affects every aspect of their life. Our mission is to help the kids learn how to overcome these struggles and learn how to become more confident.

Come be apart of this year's BeYOU retreat and enjoy a weekend of confidence building, dance parties, swimming, making new friends, and having a lot of fun!

The Retreat!




Located at the Chateau de Vie in Tempe, Arizona (shown above), this retreat will give you guidance on becoming more confident and leave you with life-long friends!
